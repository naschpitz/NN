1. Train ISIC:

./build/NN-CLI --config examples/ISIC-MIL10k/train/isic_cnn_config.json --mode train --input-type image --samples examples/ISIC-MIL10k/train/samples.json --log-level info




2. Test ISIC with its own training data (use the auto-generated model from examples/ISIC-MIL10k/train/output/):

./build/NN-CLI --config examples/ISIC-MIL10k/train/output/trained_E-*_S-*_L-*.json --mode test --input-type image --samples examples/ISIC-MIL10k/train/samples.json --log-level info




3. Train MNIST:

./build/NN-CLI --config examples/MNIST/mnist_cnn_config.json --mode train --idx-data examples/MNIST/train/train-images.idx3-ubyte --idx-labels examples/MNIST/train/train-labels.idx1-ubyte --log-level info




4. Test MNIST (use the auto-generated model from examples/MNIST/train/output/):

./build/NN-CLI --config examples/MNIST/train/output/trained_E-*_S-*_L-*.json --mode test --idx-data examples/MNIST/test/t10k-images.idx3-ubyte --idx-labels examples/MNIST/test/t10k-labels.idx1-ubyte --log-level info
